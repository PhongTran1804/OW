PLAY BASIC RULES

CHARACTERS

Villagers

The Villagers are a team seeking to kill all of the Werewolves. A Villager is a player character with no abilities, but the Village also includes any role not on the same team of the Werewolves, like the Guard, the Seer, the Witch, and others.

Investigator

The Investigator is a Villager, and is told whether or not either of the players sitting next to them has awoken during the night. Only a yes or no is given. The Moderator doesn't indicate which one awoke. The Insomniac must wake last during the night.

Reavealor

The Revealor is a Villager who can shield one of the other players from Werewolf attacks. On the first night, the Priest chooses who should receive protection. The next attempt to kill the selected player fails. The night after that attempt (or if the protected player is lynched / killed by the Witch, the Hunter, etc), the Priest can choose a different player to shield

Werewolf

The Werewolves are a team working against the Villagers. The Werewolves collectively choose a player to kill each night. Each night the Moderator will wake them up as a group and allow them to choose a victim to kill for that night. The Moderator tells the victim as the day phase starts.

Alphawolf

The Alpha Werewolf is a member of the Werewolf team. Whenever an Alpha Werewolf is killed he chooses another player. The chosen player dies as well.

Mysticwolf

The Mystic Wolf wakes up withthe other werewolves- but also has the Seer-l;ike ability to view the role card of one other player
